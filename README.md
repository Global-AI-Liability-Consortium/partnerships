# Institutional Partnership Program

The Global AI Liability Consortium advances AI liability through long-term institutional partnerships that strengthen the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

The Institutional Partnership Program provides organizations with meaningful opportunities to contribute expertise, research, professional practice, and institutional leadership to the development of GAILC's Knowledge Hubs.

Partnerships are built around shared knowledge creation rather than sponsorship.

> **Liability is where AI disciplines converge.**

---

# Purpose

The Institutional Partnership Program exists to connect GAILC with organizations that share a commitment to advancing AI accountability.

Partners contribute to the development of enduring institutional assets that benefit organizations, professionals, researchers, policymakers, and the public.

Together, these partnerships strengthen both the individual Knowledge Hubs and the Global AI Liability Consortium as a whole.

---

# Partnership Philosophy

GAILC does not seek organizations to endorse its work.

GAILC seeks institutions willing to help build the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

Every partnership is founded on shared expertise, long-term collaboration, and the advancement of practical knowledge.

---

# Founding Knowledge Partnerships

Each Knowledge Hub may have a single Founding Knowledge Partner.

Founding Knowledge Partners collaborate with GAILC to help establish and develop a permanent institutional asset within the Consortium.

Their contributions may include:

- Research
- Professional expertise
- Practical guidance
- Assessment frameworks
- Assessment tools
- Educational resources
- Publications
- Case studies
- Standards contributions
- Institutional collaboration

Organizations may become the Founding Knowledge Partner for more than one Knowledge Hub where their expertise aligns with GAILC's mission.

---

# Institutional Relationship

Knowledge Hubs remain permanent institutional assets of the Global AI Liability Consortium.

Founding Knowledge Partners help develop those assets through expertise, leadership, and long-term collaboration.

The Consortium provides the institutional framework within which every Knowledge Hub continues to evolve.

---

# Partnership Principles

Every partnership should:

- Advance AI liability knowledge.
- Strengthen one or more Knowledge Hubs.
- Contribute enduring institutional value.
- Benefit the global AI community.
- Support GAILC's mission and principles.

---

# Who May Partner With GAILC

The Consortium welcomes collaboration with organizations including:

- Professional Services Firms
- Audit & Assurance Firms
- Insurance Organizations
- Technology Companies
- Standards Organizations
- Research Institutions
- Universities
- Law Firms
- Regulatory Bodies
- Industry Associations
- Public Interest Organizations

---

# Looking Forward

As GAILC grows, its Institutional Partnership Program will continue to expand through organizations that share a commitment to advancing AI liability as a permanent field of professional knowledge.

---

# Closing Statement

Institutional partnerships are not an end.

They are the means through which enduring knowledge is created, strengthened, and shared.

Together, GAILC and its partners build the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.
