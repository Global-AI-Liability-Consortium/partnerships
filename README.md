# Institutional Partnership Program

The Global AI Liability Consortium advances AI liability through long-term institutional partnerships that strengthen the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

The Institutional Partnership Program provides organizations with meaningful opportunities to contribute expertise, professional practice, research, and institutional leadership to the development of GAILC's Knowledge Hubs.

Institutional partnerships are founded on shared knowledge creation, long-term collaboration, and the development of enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

> **Liability is where AI disciplines converge.**

---

# Purpose

The Institutional Partnership Program exists to connect GAILC with organizations that share a commitment to advancing AI accountability.

Partners collaborate with GAILC to help build permanent institutional assets that benefit organizations, professionals, researchers, policymakers, and the public.

Together, these partnerships strengthen individual Knowledge Hubs while advancing the Global AI Liability Consortium as a whole.

---

# Partnership Philosophy

GAILC seeks institutions committed to advancing AI liability through long-term collaboration, shared expertise, and the development of enduring knowledge infrastructure.

Every partnership is founded upon a shared commitment to practical knowledge, institutional excellence, and professional advancement.

Institutional partnerships are intended to create resources that continue serving the global AI community long after their initial development.

---

# Founding Knowledge Partnerships

Each Knowledge Hub may have a single Founding Knowledge Partner.

A Founding Knowledge Partner collaborates with GAILC to establish and continuously develop a permanent institutional asset within the Consortium.

Organizations may become the Founding Knowledge Partner for more than one Knowledge Hub where their expertise aligns with GAILC's mission.

Knowledge Hubs remain permanent institutional assets of the Global AI Liability Consortium.

# Institutional Relationship

Knowledge Hubs remain permanent institutional assets of the Global AI Liability Consortium.

Founding Knowledge Partners co-develop the institutional assets that comprise each Knowledge Hub through expertise, leadership, research, and long-term collaboration.

The Consortium provides the institutional framework within which every Knowledge Hub continues to evolve.

---

# Partnership Principles

Every institutional partnership should:

- Advance AI liability knowledge.
- Strengthen one or more Knowledge Hubs.
- Develop enduring institutional assets.
- Contribute practical value to organizations and professionals.
- Support interdisciplinary collaboration.
- Strengthen the global AI liability ecosystem.
- Advance GAILC's mission and institutional principles.
